Copilot Now Edits Your Word Docs Directly!

💡 What's New?

Forget copy-paste and back-and-forth. Copilot in Word can now edit your document directly from the chat pane — just describe what you want and watch it happen. Every change is fully reversible, so there's zero risk of losing your work.

Microsoft Word — Copilot Chat Pane

🌟 Why You'll Love It

Staring at a blank page? Just open Word, type a prompt like "Write a welcome letter for a new patient" and Copilot will draft it for you — right inside the document. No mode switching, no fuss.

📋 How To Use It — Step by Step
  1. Open Microsoft Word and create a new document (or open an existing one).
  2. Click the Copilot icon in the ribbon at the top of the screen.
  3. Type your request in the chat box — for example: "Draft a project update for our team."
  4. Copilot will write the text and insert it directly into your document.
  5. Review the result — use the Accept or Discard buttons, or ask Copilot to refine it further.

🆕 Feature 2 of 2

PowerPoint Just Got a Whole Lot Smarter 🌟

💡 What's New?

Copilot is now your personal PowerPoint presentation assistant. You can build, edit, and polish entire slide decks just by chatting with it. It can generate slides, fix layouts, adjust the tone, and even pull in content from your emails and files.

Microsoft PowerPoint — Copilot Chat Pane
🎨
Screenshot: PowerPoint open with the Copilot pane on the right side, showing a slide creation prompt
Replace with actual screenshot from your PowerPoint app
🌟 Why You'll Love It

No more spending an hour formatting slides. Just tell Copilot what you need — "Make a 5-slide summary of our Q1 results" — and it builds the deck for you. You can then ask it to tweak the design, shorten a slide, or change the tone. It's like having a presentation designer on call 24/7!

📋 How To Use It — Step by Step
  1. Open Microsoft PowerPoint and start a new presentation or open an existing one.
  2. Click the Copilot icon in the ribbon to open the chat pane on the right.
  3. Type a request, such as: "Create a 5-slide presentation about our new patient onboarding process."
  4. Copilot will generate the slides automatically — review them and ask for any changes.
  5. Try follow-up prompts like: "Make slide 3 shorter" or "Change the tone to be more friendly."

Copilot is Now on Your Home Screen!

💡 What's New

Microsoft has launched new Copilot home screen and lock screen widgets for both iPhone and Android phones! Plus, iPhone users on supported models can now set their Action Button to launch Copilot instantly. One tap and you're chatting with AI — no unlocking, no searching for the app!

 iPhone (iOS) 🧩 Android
Mobile — Copilot Home Screen Widget
📱
Screenshot: iPhone home screen showing the Copilot widget next to an Android home screen with the same widget
Replace with actual screenshots from iOS and Android devices
 iPhone Setup — Step by Step
  1. Long-press on an empty area of your Home Screen until the icons start to jiggle.
  2. Tap the "+" button in the top-left corner.
  3. Search for "Copilot" in the widget search bar.
  4. Select your preferred widget size and tap "Add Widget."
  5. Bonus: Go to Settings > Action Button (on supported iPhone models) and assign it to open Copilot.

🧩 Android Setup — Step by Step
  1. Long-press on an empty space on your Android home screen.
  2. Tap "Widgets" from the menu that appears at the bottom.
  3. Scroll through the list to find "Copilot."
  4. Long-press the widget and drag it to your preferred spot on the home screen.
  5. Tap the widget to instantly start a chat, voice conversation, or visual search with Copilot!
